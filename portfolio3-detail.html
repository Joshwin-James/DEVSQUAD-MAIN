Project Title

The DEVSQUAD Esports Championship 2025 will feature a series of online and offline qualifiers leading to the final tournament, where top teams will compete for a grand prize. The tournament will focus on popular games like League of Legends, Dota 2, and Valorant. It will be streamed live to global audiences, with high-quality production, engaging commentary, and interactive features such as live fan voting, audience polls, and giveaways.
